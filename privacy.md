# Privacy Policy — TikTokMap

Last updated: March 2026

## Data We Collect
- Email address and first name (for account creation)
- Places and addresses you save in the app
- TikTok video links you share with the app

## How We Use Your Data
- To provide the TikTokMap service
- To save your places securely in the cloud via Firebase
- To extract location information from TikTok videos via TikTok's API

## Data Storage
Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties.

## TikTok API Usage
We use TikTok's API solely to read video metadata (title, description) in order to extract place and address information. We do not store TikTok user data.

## Your Rights
You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Profile page.
